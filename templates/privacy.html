Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

At Spendly, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Service, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using Spendly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited, actions taken, and session duration for improving the Service.
  • Device information — browser type and operating system, collected automatically via server logs.

We do not collect payment details, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account information.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to operate and improve the Spendly Service. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Display your expense history and summaries.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service.
  • Respond to support requests or technical issues.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured database. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using a cryptographically secure algorithm before being saved.

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use minimal third-party services to operate — such as hosting providers or web fonts. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms that share data, or any service that tracks you across other websites.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the Spendly support channel.
